About Me

Hello! My name is Ratna Kirti, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science at Knox College. Over the years, I have immersed myself in a variety of programming languages and technologies, including CUDA, Elixir, OpenMPI, Docker, SQL, Haskell, Python, C#, Java, JavaScript, HTML, and PHP. My versatility allows me to adapt to different languages seamlessly, but my true passion lies in web development.

One of my proudest achievements is developing a website for a perfumery business, Deo Exotic Attar and Perfumes, which supports global payments and has significantly enhanced the business's online presence.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have gained valuable industry experience as a Server Administrator Intern at the Irrigation and Water Resources Department. This role has honed my skills in server management and provided me with a deeper understanding of the practical applications of my studies.

I am always eager to collaborate with others on innovative projects and contribute my expertise to a dynamic team. Whether it's building robust web applications, managing servers, or exploring new programming paradigms, I am committed to delivering high-quality solutions and continuously expanding my knowledge.

Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to the possibility of working together!
